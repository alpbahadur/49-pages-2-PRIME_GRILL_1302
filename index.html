A Legacy of Excellence

Welcome to PRIME GRILL 1302, where tradition meets excellence in the vibrant neighborhood of Condado, San Juan. Our family-style steakhouse is built on a foundation of passion for premium quality meats and genuine Puerto Rican hospitality.

We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we source only the finest hand-cut prime meats and the freshest local produce from Puerto Rico's best farms and suppliers.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is crafted with care, combining time-honored techniques with creative culinary artistry to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.
